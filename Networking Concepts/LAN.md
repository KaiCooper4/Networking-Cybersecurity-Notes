Local access network - Devices on this network connect to each other through one or more switches;

PAN - Bluetooth

**Topologies**
Hybrid/Ring Topology - No bottlenecks; one packet at a time; flaw: one connection breaks, entire network fails.
Bus Topology - Packets move from left to right; Bottleneck occurs when too much data comes from one computer; unreliable but cost-efficient; connected to a backbone cable.
Star Topology - Most expensive but most common; connected to a central switch/hub; if switch breaks, the entire network breaks.
Mesh Topology - Every device connects to every other device; highly redundant, expensive.
