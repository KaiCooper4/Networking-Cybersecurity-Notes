FSB/hypertransport: CPU <-> Northbridge
Memory Bus: Memory <-> Northbridge
Expansion Buses: every other bus
-ISA: Industry Standard Architecture (16 bits)
-PCI: Peripheral Component Interconnect (32 bits)
\--PCIx: eXtended (32 bits)
\--PCIe: express, most common (serial)
\--mini-PCI: for laptops
-AGP: Accelerated Graphics Port, displays & graphics (32 bits)
-AMR & CNR: sound cards & modems
-PCMCIA: laptop expansion port
